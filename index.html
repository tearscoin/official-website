ABOUT

Mental health is very important nowadays, but sometimes, due to the fear of being judged by others, it can be very difficult to ask for help.

Meme coins have proven that they can create incredibly strong communities, and from this fact, $Tears was born.

The goal is to harness the power of communities made up of people from all over the world to share their life experiences with those who need them the most.

Do you feel good about yourself, or are you not very good at providing moral support by sharing your experience? Don't worry, we’re not all the same, and the world is beautiful because of this! How can you still contribute to doing good? Buy $Tears, help increase its value to enable concrete assistance for those in need.

ROADMAP

1 - Denial

$Tears has no presale, and the founders are not multimillionaire influencers living in Dubai. The initial liquidity isn't much, but we believe in this project. That's why, as $Tears grows, we will continue to increase the locked liquidity.

2 - Anger

Stake your $Tears. During the anger phase, stability is needed. Secure your $Tears for greater peace of mind in facing your problems. And remember, we are here to stay by your side.

3 - Bargaining

Every 15 days, a snapshot is taken. 5 addresses will receive a voucher to be used for a therapy session. The more $Tears grows, the more addresses (=pepole) we can reach!

4 - Depression

During the depression phase, your mind may feel a bit burnt out. A mental healing journey takes time, and at this stage, $Tears will become an important tool for people. Upon reaching this phase, the Team will perform a buyback equal of 10% of the staked $Tears and burn them. As your self-awareness grows, your $Tears become more valuable.

5 - New Beginning

This is the most ambitious part of the project. The light at the end of the tunnel. It will be revealed in due time. A hint? The more you help, the more $Tears you earn. Make sure that all the $Tears in your life haven’t been wasted.

Of course, one of our goals is also to be listed on the most important CEXs.

This will be possible thanks to your support!
