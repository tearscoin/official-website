3. Why We Chose a 1 Billion Token Supply

The decision to set $Tears supply at 1 Billion tokens was made for several strategic reasons:

  • Feasibility of Raising Funds: With this supply, we can maintain a launch price that is accessible to new investors and attractive for presale participants, making it easier to raise the necessary funds to support the project.
  • Competitive Launch Price: We are targeting a specific launch price per token. With a supply of 1 Billion, this price is sustainable and doesn’t lead to an unrealistic initial market cap, balancing the token’s attractiveness for new investors and presale holders.
  • Manageable Liquidity: This supply makes liquidity distribution more manageable, ensuring price stability post-launch, reducing volatility, and increasing investor confidence.
  • Ideal Supply-Demand Ratio: Maintaining a 1 Billion supply allows a good balance between supply and demand, avoiding token value dilution and keeping it accessible.
  • Comparability with Other Projects: Many successful projects have adopted similar supplies, positioning $Tears in a competitive range, making it easier to attract investors.
